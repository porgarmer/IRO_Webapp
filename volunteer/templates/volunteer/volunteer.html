WHY VOLUNTEER WITH US?

Volunteering at Island Rescue Organization is a rewarding experience where your time directly impacts the lives of animals in need. From caring for rescues to assisting at events, every hour spent helps bring love and safety to animals waiting for their forever homes.

    Benefits of Volunteering:

  • Make a direct impact on animal welfare.
  • Be part of a supportive, compassionate community.
  • Gain hands-on experience in animal care and advocacy.
