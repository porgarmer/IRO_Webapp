Camp IRO

Location: Cebu City


Visitors allowed: Yes (per appointment basis; no walk-ins)


Camp IRO is the main rescue center that serves as the primary home for the majority of IRO's rescued dogs,

while also providing shelter to a few cats. It is the hub where adoptable dogs are prepared for and returned from each Adoption Fair.

Additionally, the center welcomes volunteer days and accommodates student short studies,

hands-on opportunities to engage with the animals and support their care.



IRO 'O' Cattery

Location: Mandaue

Visitors allowed: Yes (per appointment basis; no walk-ins)


IRO 'O' Cattery is the main cattery, which also serves as IRO's headquarters, is home to the majority of the organization's

rescued cats, including special-needs felines affectionately known as "Cats with Disabilities." It is the central hub where

adoptable cats are prepared for and returned from each Adoption Fair.



IRO Tuesday Foster Home

Location: Consolacion


Visitors allowed: No


IRO Tuesday Foster Home provides a safe haven for animals awaiting adoption or the completion of travel documents.

It also serves as a rehabilitation space for dogs transitioning from the clinic to the main shelter or their adoptive homes.

Additionally, it temporarily houses dogs undergoing treatment or those with regular veterinary appointments.



IRO Lolo Ali Center (LAC)

Location: Barili

Visitors allowed: Seasonal (still at an appointment basis)


IRO Lolo Ali Center serves as a retirement home for dogs, including some undergoing training.

It also provides shelter for a few farm animals and rescued wildlife.

Additionally, it is a haven for special-needs dogs and puppies, offering them the care and support they need.
