OUR VISION, MISSION, AND GOAL

VISION

To have a community where animals are treated humanely; we envision a society where people advocate for the animals' rights and treat them with kindness and respect.

MISSION

Promote responsible pet ownership and humane treatment of all animals through cooperation, outreach, and education. Pursue the development and enforcement of humane and effective animal welfare laws, and provide a healthy, loving, truly no-kill sanctuary for these animals.

GOAL

i. Rehome rehabilitated rescued animals and make room for fosters.

ii. Engage the public and give opportunities for involvement through volunteerism. Mobilize volunteers to pursue the organization's mission.

iii. Rally the community towards advocating for animals' rights (private, public, corporate sectors, the academe, civic groups, and individuals).
